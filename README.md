# 🎓 Gayajii Teacher Hub

Welcome to **Gayajii Teacher Hub** – your one-stop solution for quality education and teaching opportunities.

---

## 📌 About Us

Gayajii Teacher Hub is an educational platform dedicated to connecting **students and teachers** in the most efficient way.  
We provide multiple types of tuition services and also help teachers find suitable job opportunities.

---

## 🚀 Our Services

### 👨‍🏫 For Students:
- Home Tuitions (Personal teachers at your home)
- Coaching Center Tuitions
- School Tuitions
- Subject-wise expert teachers
- Quality education support

---

### 👩‍🏫 For Teachers:
- Part-time teaching jobs
- Full-time teaching jobs in schools
- Opportunities in coaching centers
- Home tuition teaching opportunities

---

## 🔄 What We Do

- We **provide teachers to students** based on their needs  
- We **help teachers find jobs** in schools and coaching institutes  
- We act as a **bridge between students and teachers**
